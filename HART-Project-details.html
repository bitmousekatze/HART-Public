01 — OVERVIEW

What is HART?

HART is a curated digital art gallery and collector community built on Solana. Artists submit original work, curators approve it, and collectors buy, gift, and trade using the HART token. Every transaction burns a portion of the supply on-chain, creating permanent deflationary pressure tied to real human activity.

Core Mission: A human-curated space where digital art has real provenance, real value, and a community that builds together — not an algorithmic feed. Every artwork is approved by human curators. No AI. No Exceptions. Ever.
🎨

HART.GALLERY

The core platform. Artists submit work, collectors purchase with HART token, and every sale burns a percentage of the supply on-chain. Curated, gated community.

🌍

HART.WORLD

Real-world cleanup integration. Every verified beach cleanup, park restoration, or environmental action burns HART from a dedicated 500M token treasury. GPS-verified, permanently recorded.

02 — PLATFORM FEATURES

Complete Ecosystem

Dual Payment Rails

Buyers can pay in fiat via Stripe Connect or in HART token via Phantom wallet. Both produce identical on-chain burn records. Artists are the merchant of record for fiat sales with a 6% application fee automatically deducted.

  • Stripe Connect integration for USD payments
  • Phantom wallet for direct HART token transactions
  • Automatic burn notarization on Solana
  • Artist payouts via connected bank accounts
  • Platform fee enforcement at checkout

Community Canvas

A collaborative 48×48 pixel grid where collectors claim sections and paint their vision. Burns HART to claim, requires wallet ownership verification, and creates collective art visible to the entire community.

  • 48×48 pixel collaborative canvas
  • Burn HART to claim individual sections
  • Real-time painting with wallet verification
  • History tracking and provenance
  • Community voting on featured works

Gifting System

Send artwork to other collectors with a personal message. Recipient gets ownership transfer recorded on-chain. Burns 1% of artwork's current value as the gift fee.

  • Wallet-to-wallet transfers with messages
  • 1% HART burn on every gift
  • On-chain provenance tracking
  • Gift history visible on artwork pages

Artist Type Expansion

Beyond digital 2D art. Supporting traditional painters, code-based generative artists, animators, pixel artists, and photographers. Physical artwork + digital rights model.

  • Traditional/physical artists with digital reproduction rights
  • Code-based generative art with source verification
  • Animation (hand-drawn, stop-motion, 3D)
  • Enhanced pixel art categorization
  • Photography (conditional, human-focused)

Why This Stack?

Built entirely with Claude Code over 6 weeks. The simplicity of vanilla JavaScript with Supabase backend enabled rapid prototyping and iteration. No build step means instant deploys. Solana provides fast, cheap on-chain burns. Stripe Connect handles fiat with minimal friction.

Claude Code's Role: Every database schema, every RPC function, every burn mechanism, and every UI component was designed and implemented through Claude Code. The platform went from concept to working prototype in weeks, not months.

Key Technical Challenges

MULTI-WALLET ARCHITECTURE

Supporting both Phantom (for HART token) and Stripe (for fiat) required careful coordination. Every transaction must produce a verifiable on-chain burn record regardless of payment method.

BURN RECORD VERIFICATION

Every platform fee, gift, and canvas claim burns HART and creates a permanent Solana transaction. The system validates burns, stores transaction hashes, and displays them publicly on the burn ledger.

REAL-WORLD GPS CLEANUP

hart.world verifies GPS coordinates, timestamps, and photo evidence for beach cleanups. Admin reviews submissions, then executes burns from the 500M treasury wallet. Every cleanup is plotted on a public map.

3D GALLERY RENDERING

hart.spaces loads a collector's entire artwork collection and renders it as textured planes in explorable Three.js environments. No new uploads required — just visualization of existing ownership data.

04 — ECONOMICS

Deflationary by Design

HART is designed to become scarcer with every transaction. The more activity on the platform, the fewer tokens remain in circulation. This creates natural value appreciation tied to community engagement.

🔥

ART SALES

6% platform fee burned on every purchase (both fiat and token). Higher value art = more HART removed from supply.

🎁

GIFTING

1% of artwork's current value burned when gifted. Encourages collectors to share work while maintaining deflationary pressure.

🌊

CLEANUP BURNS

Every verified beach cleanup, park restoration, or environmental action burns HART from the 500M treasury. Real-world impact drives digital scarcity.

Unique Value Proposition: HART is the only platform where the token burns when the world gets cleaner. The person cleaning a beach in California and the artist selling a painting in Tokyo are on the same team — both driving deflationary pressure that benefits all holders.
