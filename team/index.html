The Team

Five people. Five handles. The ones who actually do the work.

Who's behind it

HART was never meant to be about us. The art is the point. The artists are the point. We're the people who stayed up too late writing the code, drawing the mascots, and answering the messages.

Why handles

Everyone on the team uses a handle. Not because we're hiding — most of us are reachable in public Discord every day — but because HART is not a personal brand.

These pages exist so anyone considering uploading their work, joining the community, or just trying to understand who's running this thing has a real human voice to read — not a generic "About Us" boilerplate.
