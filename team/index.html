The Team

The people behind HART. Handles, not legal names — by choice.

Why handles?

Everyone on the team uses a handle. Not because we're hiding — most of us are reachable in public Discord every day — but because HART was never meant to be about us. The art is the point. The artists are the point. We're the people who stayed up too late writing the code, drawing the mascots, and answering the messages.

These pages exist so anyone considering uploading their work, joining the community, or just trying to understand who's running this thing has a real human voice to read — not a generic "About Us" boilerplate.

What you won't find here

  • Real names, photos, or contact details. The handles and Discord are how to reach us.
  • Wallet addresses or financial figures. Those live on the live platform, not in design docs.
  • An "advisory board" or fake titles. The team is the team — five people who actually do the work.
